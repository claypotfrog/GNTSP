<p spacebelow=5>Verbs can be visually marked for Tense, Voice, Mood, and Person/Number using "Verb Feature Filters".</p>
<p spacebelow=5><img src="binfile:help/filters.png"></p>
<p spacebelow=10>Filters are turned OFF by default.  Enable them in the Settings on the Read page.  See <b>Verb Feature Filters</b> on the <b>About</b> page for more information.</p>
<p spacebelow=10>The program tracks when you study vocabulary and shows study statistics.  Statistics can be turned on or off in the Settings on the Vocabulary page.</p>
<p spacebelow=10>Textual Apparatus shows and hides in no extra time.</p>
<p spacebelow=10>Vocabulary Lists from Textbooks and Vocabulary Frequency Lists can be imported by name.</p>
